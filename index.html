About Us

Maxon Infrastructure Private Limited was founded in 2013 under the name Max Decors. In 2019, we introduced two new segments, Maxon Console and Max Interior, which played a pivotal role in the company's evolution into Maxon Infrastructure Private Limited. Previously operating as a non-private limited entity, the company underwent a transformation and is now a private limited company. During this transition, we successfully acquired trademarks for both Maxon and Max Decors.

Maxon Infrastructure, under the visionary leadership of CEO Nazeer, is more than just a manufacturer – we are creators of Modular Kitchen, Wardrobe, Loft, Pooja unit, Wall Panelling, TV Unit, Zebra Blinds, Roller Blinds, Roman Blinds, and Wallpaper solutions that transcend functionality to become expressions of your unique style. We firmly believe that these interior elements have the power to infuse warmth and personality into a space, transforming it into a haven that resonates with your individuality.
